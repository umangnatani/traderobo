How does Robinhood Authentication work?

When you authenticate to Robinhood with your UserName and Password, a MFA token is sent to your registered phone via text.
Once MFA token is verified, a Robinhood access token is generated and stored with 128 bit encryption and expiry date.
The Robinhood UserName and Password are never stored and once the token expires, you will need to re-authenticate.

What is "Use Price Weighted Strategy" in a Pie Order.

If you select this option, the weight of stocks in your Pie will be added or reduced based on the real time price of the stock.
The stock which is down will have slightly more weight whereas stocks which are up will have less weight depending on the percentage change (up or down).
